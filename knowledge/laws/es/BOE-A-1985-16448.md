# Ley 14/1985, de 28 de junio, por la que se regula el Consejo Nacional de la Juventud de Cataluña

## Summary
Regulates the Consejo Nacional de la Juventud de Cataluña (CNJC) as a public-law entity that represents Catalan youth associations and serves as interlocutor between young people and the Generalitat. Defines membership categories, governance structure (Asamblea General, Presidencia, Secretariado), and funding sources. Derogates the 1979 decree that originally created the CNJC.

## Key Provisions
- CNJC is a public-law entity with its own legal personality
- Promotes youth participation in public decisions
- Acts as interlocutor between youth associations and Generalitat
- Two membership tiers: full members and adhered members
- Full members require two years of existence and seven sponsoring members
- Asamblea General meets at least once annually
- Two-year term for Secretariado and President
- Funded by member contributions, Generalitat grants, and donations

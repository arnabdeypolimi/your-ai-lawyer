# Ley 23/1983, de 21 de noviembre, de Politica Territorial

## Summary
Defines the framework for Catalan territorial planning through four instruments: the General Territorial Plan, Partial Territorial Plans, Sectoral Territorial Plans, and Territorial Directing Plans. Classifies territory into depressed, development, and congested zones and enables subsidies, fiscal incentives, and special levies to rebalance economic activity and population. Urbanism plans must be coherent with these territorial instruments.

## Key Provisions
- Plan Territorial General sets framework for all territorial actions
- Zones classified as depressed, developing, or congested
- Partial plans have minimum comarcal scope
- Sectoral plans elaborated by each Generalitat department
- Territorial directing plans have supramunicipal scope
- Protected natural spaces cannot be transformed in use or nature
- Subsidies and tax incentives for activities in depressed zones
- Special levies possible on activities aggravating congestion
- Comisión de Coordinación replaced by Comisión de Política Territorial y Urbanismo
- Provisional approval requires two-thirds of affected municipalities

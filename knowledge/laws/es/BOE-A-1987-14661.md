# Ley 12/1987, de 28 de mayo, de regulación del transporte de viajeros por carretera mediante vehículos de motor

## Summary
Establishes Catalonia's framework for road passenger transport, classifying services as public/private/official and urban/interurban, regular/discretional. Introduces the figure of the "operador de transporte" and the concept of zonal transport concessions alongside traditional linear concessions. Distinguishes urban (within classified urban land) from interurban transport and requires quality licences based on professional fitness criteria consistent with EEC principles.

## Key Provisions
- Public/private/official classification by service nature
- Urban vs interurban classification by "suelo urbano" boundary
- Regular vs discretional classification by predetermined itinerary
- Introduces the transport operator figure for discretional services
- Quality licences verify operator honour and professional aptitude
- Zonal concessions allowed alongside traditional linear concessions
- Plan de Transportes de Viajeros coordinates the entire network
- Generalitat may create monopolies only by specific legislation
- Special regulation for light vehicles and sanitary transport
- Inspection service and graduated sanction regime

# Ley 4/1980, de 16 de diciembre de 1980, de creación del Instituto Catalán del Suelo

## Summary
Creates the Catalan Land Institute (Institut Català del Sòl) as a public entity of the Generalitat, tasked with promoting urbanized land, public housing, and urban renewal. The Institute acts as the urbanism administration for executing planning, can expropriate land, and operates under both public and private law to combat speculation and ensure the right to decent housing.

## Key Provisions
- Institute is a public-law entity attached to Department of Territorial Policy
- Promotes urbanized land, public housing, and urban rehabilitation
- May acquire land including through expropriation as beneficiary
- Governed by Board of Administration and a Director
- Manages rental deposits registry for urban properties
- Subject to public-sector contracting legislation
- Administrative acts subject to appeal before the Territorial Policy Councillor
- Finances from transferred INUR assets, budget allocations, and own revenues

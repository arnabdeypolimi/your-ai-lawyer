# Ley 12/1985, de 13 de junio, de Espacios Naturales

## Summary
Establishes Catalonia's framework for protection, conservation, and management of natural spaces, biodiversity, and landscape. Creates the Plan de Espacios de Interés Natural (PEIN) and four categories of specially protected spaces: parques nacionales, parajes naturales de interés nacional, reservas naturales, and parques naturales. Integrates EU Natura 2000 (ZEC and ZEPA) designations automatically into PEIN.

## Key Provisions
- Four categories of protected spaces with specific regimes
- National parks and nature reserves declared by law
- Plan de Espacios de Interés Natural with indefinite validity
- Strict protection for flora, fauna, rocks, and fossils
- Wetlands preserved against activities causing recession
- Protection buffer zones around lakes, reservoirs, and coast
- Natura 2000 ZEC and ZEPA integrated automatically into PEIN
- Consejo de Protección de la Naturaleza created as advisory body
- Right of preemption for Generalitat on large properties in protected spaces
- Public action to require enforcement of the law
- Fines graduated from €300 up to €3,005 for environmental infractions

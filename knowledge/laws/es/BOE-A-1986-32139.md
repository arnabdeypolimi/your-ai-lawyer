# Ley 9/1986, de 10 de noviembre, de Cuerpos de Funcionarios de la Generalidad

## Summary
Creates the professional civil-service bodies of the Generalitat of Catalonia, organized by level of academic qualification into four groups (A/B/C/D). Also structures the Cos de Mossos d'Esquadra into basic and intermediate scales with agent, cabo, and sargent ranks. Establishes integration rules for civil servants transferred from other administrations while respecting their acquired rights.

## Key Provisions
- Four civil-service bodies matching Groups A, B, C, and D
- Titulación Superior (Group A) requires Doctor, Licenciado, Ingeniero, Arquitecto
- Diplomatura (Group B) requires university diploma or technical engineering
- Técnico Especialistas (Group C) requires bachillerato or FP second grade
- Auxiliar Técnico (Group D) requires graduado escolar or FP first grade
- Cos de Mossos d'Esquadra with basic and intermediate scales
- Mossos selection via public competition + Escuela de Policía training
- Internal promotion for higher ranks requires two years' seniority
- Transferred civil servants integrated respecting acquired rights

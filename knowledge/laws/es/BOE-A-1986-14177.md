# Ley 5/1986, de 17 de abril, de creación de la Entidad Autónoma de Juegos y Apuestas de la Generalidad

## Summary
Creates the Entidad Autónoma de Juegos y Apuestas de la Generalidad (EAJA) as a commercial public entity responsible for organizing, managing, and commercializing games and lotteries reserved to the Generalitat. Revenues are earmarked for social welfare, particularly programs for the elderly, disabled, and childcare centres. Later transformed into the Loteries de Catalunya SAU public company in 2021.

## Key Provisions
- Public entity with its own legal personality for game management
- Governed by Consejo de Administración and Consejero Delegado
- Organizes and commercializes games reserved to the Generalitat
- Can use administrative enforcement for revenue collection
- Income earmarked for Bienestar Social department programs
- Funds elderly care, disability support, and childcare centres
- Later transformed into a single-shareholder public company
- Derogated in 2021 by Decreto-ley 16/2021 — remains for compiled historical context

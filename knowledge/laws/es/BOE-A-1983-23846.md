# Ley 15/1983, de 14 de julio, de higiene y control alimentarios

## Summary
Establishes Catalonia's framework for food hygiene and control across all phases of the food cycle, from production through sale. Creates the Sanitary Registry of Food Industries and Products, mandates inspections, and defines infractions with sanctions up to 50 million pesetas for very serious cases affecting public health. Largely repealed except for articles 8–11 by Ley 7/2003 and Ley 18/2009.

## Key Provisions
- Covers all food industries operating in Catalonia
- Mandatory registration in Sanitary Registry before commercialization
- Registered sanitary number must appear on packaging
- Authorizations revalidated every five years
- Central Laboratory for Food Control structures analytical network
- Inspectors are considered agents of authority
- Infractions leve/grave/muy grave with fines up to 50 million pesetas
- Complementary sanctions include closure and loss of public aid
- Delegation possible to local entities for inspection functions

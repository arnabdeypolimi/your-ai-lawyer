# Ley 12/1981, de 24 de diciembre, por la que se establecen normas de protección de los espacios de especial interés natural afectados por actividades extractivas

## Summary
Regulates mining and extractive activities within spaces of special natural interest in Catalonia by requiring restoration programs, environmental bonds, and binding impact assessments. Operators must submit restoration plans with their mining applications, deposit a financial guarantee, and face administrative sanctions up to €1 million for infractions. The law applies the "restoration principle" to make extractive activity compatible with environmental protection.

## Key Provisions
- Mining applications in listed natural spaces require restoration program
- Binding environmental report from Direcció General de Política Territorial
- Authorization denied when restoration is economically unviable or impossible
- Mandatory financial bond before commencing extraction
- Bond amount minimum 400,000 pesetas per hectare or 25% of restoration budget
- Annual bond review with CPI indexation
- Infractions classified as leve, grave, or muy grave
- Fines up to €1,000,000 for very serious infractions
- Coercive fines independent of sanctions may be imposed

# Ley 15/1984, de 20 de marzo, del juego

## Summary
Regulates all casinos, games, and betting activities in Catalonia under the Generalitat's exclusive statutory competence. Establishes a catalogue of authorized games, the discretionary authorization regime for operators and venues, technical homologation of gaming material, and inspection service. Lottery is reserved to the Generalitat and operated by Loteries de Catalunya, SAU.

## Key Provisions
- Catalogue of authorized games approved by Consell Executiu
- Games involving money or economic value fall under this law
- Traditional family/social games without lucrative exploitation excluded
- Authorizations are discretionary for operators and venues
- Lottery reserved to Generalitat via Loteries de Catalunya, SAU
- Prohibited for minors, intoxicated persons, and mentally impaired
- Technical homologation of gaming machines required
- Special gaming inspection service integrates into Catalan police

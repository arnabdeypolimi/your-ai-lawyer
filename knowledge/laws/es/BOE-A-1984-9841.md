# Ley 16/1984, de 20 de marzo, del Estatuto de la Función Interventora

## Summary
Establishes the Generalitat's internal financial control function, covering oversight of all acts with economic content, public accounting direction, and internal financial auditing. Creates the Cuerpo de Intervención de la Generalitat de Cataluña with two scales (Superior and Technical), selected by competitive exams, operating with autonomy from the bodies they audit.

## Key Provisions
- Intervención General is the internal control centre for Generalitat
- Fiscalizes all acts giving rise to economic rights and obligations
- Directs public accounting across Generalitat administration
- Conducts internal financial and efficacy audits
- Cuerpo de Intervención structured in Superior and Technical scales
- Interventores act as delegates of the Interventor General with independence
- Access by competitive exam + selective training course
- Minimum half of Superior Scale posts reserved for internal promotion

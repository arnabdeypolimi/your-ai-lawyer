# Ley 20/1985, de 25 de julio, de prevención y asistencia en materia de sustancias que pueden generar dependencia

## Summary
Establishes Catalonia's comprehensive framework for prevention, treatment, and rehabilitation of dependencies on drugs, alcohol, tobacco, and other addictive substances. Regulates advertising, sale, and consumption in public venues; prohibits sale of alcohol and tobacco to minors; structures the regional assistance network; and defines infractions with fines up to 100 million pesetas for very serious violations.

## Key Provisions
- Prohibits sale of alcohol and tobacco to minors under 18
- Bans alcohol sales from 10pm to 8am in most establishments
- Prohibits "barra libre" and similar consumption-inducing promotions
- Strict advertising limits on alcohol and tobacco in public spaces
- Drug dependency classified as common illness for assistance purposes
- Voluntary treatment at hospitals with desintoxication units
- Prison programs for drug detoxification and rehabilitation
- No-smoking zones in hospitals, schools, transport, and public offices
- Priority on children and youth for preventive action
- Sanctions from 500,000 pesetas (light) to 100,000,000 pesetas (very serious)

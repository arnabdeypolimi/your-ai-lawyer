# Ley 19/1983, de 14 de julio, por la que se crea la Policía Autonómica de la Generalidad de Cataluña

## Summary
Creates the Autonomous Police of the Generalitat of Catalonia under article 13 of the Catalan Statute of Autonomy. The force's primary functions are protection of persons and property, maintenance of public order, and surveillance of Generalitat buildings. The existing Cos de Mossos d'Esquadra forms the initial core of this autonomous police force.

## Key Provisions
- Creates Catalan Autonomous Police under Statute article 13
- Primary functions: protection of persons/property, public order, building surveillance
- Cos de Mossos d'Esquadra is the initial core of the force
- Additional functions to be assigned by state Ley Orgánica

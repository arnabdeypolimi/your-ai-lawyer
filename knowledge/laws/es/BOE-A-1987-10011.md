# Ley 7/1987, de 4 de abril, por la que se establecen y regulan actuaciones públicas especiales en la «conurbación» de Barcelona y en las comarcas comprendidas dentro de su zona de influencia directa

## Summary
Restructures the governance of the Barcelona metropolitan area after the extinction of the 1974 Entidad Municipal Metropolitana. Creates two new metropolitan entities — the Entidad Metropolitana del Transporte (EMT) and the Entidad Metropolitana de Servicios Hidráulicos y Tratamiento de Residuos — and extends regional planning across five comarcas: Barcelonès, Baix Llobregat, Maresme, Vallès Occidental, and Vallès Oriental. Returns most competencies to the municipalities.

## Key Provisions
- Extinguishes the 1974 Entidad Municipal Metropolitana de Barcelona
- Creates the Entidad Metropolitana del Transporte (EMT) with 18 municipalities
- Creates the metropolitan entity for water and waste with 32 municipalities
- Regional planning covers five comarcas around Barcelona
- Plan Territorial Parcial coordinates infrastructure and land use
- Plan Intermodal de Transportes coordinates multi-modal transport
- Municipalities regain most competencies previously centralized
- Consejo metropolitano elected proportionally from municipal councils
- Barcelona has 11 seats; municipalities over 100k have 3 seats each

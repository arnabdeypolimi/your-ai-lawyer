# Ley 12/1982, de 8 de octubre, por la que se regula el procedimiento para el cambio de nombre de los Municipios de Cataluña

## Summary
Establishes the procedure for Catalan municipalities to change their official names, aimed at restoring traditional Catalan toponymy after decades of castilianization. Only municipal councils may initiate a name change, subject to ratification by the Consell Executiu of the Generalitat, with suspension possible for linguistic incorrectness or similarity to other municipalities. Derogated in 2017 by Ley 5/2017.

## Key Provisions
- Municipal name change requires two-thirds favorable vote of council members
- One-month public information period required before council decision
- Consell Executiu resolves within three months — may ratify or annul, not modify
- Governació may suspend for linguistic errors or name confusion risks
- Consell Executiu may propose rectifications to municipalities
- Homonymous municipalities must adopt a complementary distinguishing sign
- Generalitat publishes annual official list of Catalan municipalities

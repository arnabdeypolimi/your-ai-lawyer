# Ley 1/1980, de 12 de junio de 1980 por la que se declara Fiesta Nacional de Cataluña la jornada del 11 de septiembre

## Summary
Declares September 11th as the National Day of Catalonia, commemorating the loss of Catalan liberties in 1714 and the subsequent resistance movement. The law recognizes the Diada as an expression of Catalan national identity and affects all citizens of Catalonia.

## Key Provisions
- September 11 declared National Day of Catalonia
- Law enters in force on day of publication in Diari Oficial de la Generalitat

# Ley 22/1983, de 21 de noviembre, de protección del Ambiente Atmosférico

## Summary
Establishes Catalonia's framework for atmospheric pollution prevention, surveillance, and correction. Creates "Special Attention" and "Special Protection" zones with tailored action plans, requires vulnerability maps, mandates monitoring via the Red de Vigilancia, and imposes sanctions up to 15 million pesetas for very serious infractions. Public action is granted to any citizen to trigger administrative enforcement.

## Key Provisions
- Regulates emissions and immissions from fixed and mobile sources
- Creates Zones of Urgency, Special Attention, and Special Protection
- Municipal mayors may declare Zones of Urgency provisionally
- Mandatory vulnerability and capacity maps for planning reference
- Red de Vigilancia i Previsió de la Contaminació Atmosfèrica established
- Only Red-validated data is legally valid for zone declarations
- Creates Fondo for atmospheric protection fed by sanctions and taxes
- Infractions leve/grave/muy grave with fines up to 15 million pesetas
- Fines may double in Special Attention zones, triple in Protection zones
- Public action available to any person to request enforcement

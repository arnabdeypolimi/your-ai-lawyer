# Ley 21/1987, de 26 de noviembre, de incompatibilidades del personal al servicio de la Administración de la Generalidad

## Summary
Establishes the incompatibilities regime for staff serving the Catalan public administration, grounded in the principle of dedication to a single position. Defines compatible activities, the restrictive conditions for holding a second public post, limits on private activities, and retribution caps. Applies broadly to Generalitat employees, local corporations, public companies, and entities where public funds exceed 50% of capital or budget.

## Key Provisions
- Principle of dedication to a single position
- Second public post allowed only when required by public interest
- Teachers/professors may combine teaching with research or health sector
- Total retribution capped at Director General level
- Second-job earnings must not exceed principal post + 30%–50% cap
- Compatible activities include literary/artistic creation and family estate
- Elected office (Parliament, Corporations locales) compatible with limits
- Private activity prohibited if related to own department's functions
- No more than two seats on public-private boards
- Pension incompatible with active public employment (with exceptions)
- Silent denial: request deemed rejected if not answered within deadline
